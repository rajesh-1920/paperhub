Operational Flow

  1. Draft: Applicant prepares and uploads required documents.
  2. Submission: Application enters the official review queue.
  3. Verification: Assigned officer checks document quality and authenticity.
  4. Pre-Approval: Review decision is saved and visible in status tracking.
  5. Payment: Payment option is unlocked only after pre-approval.
  6. Enrollment Completion: Final state is updated after payment confirmation.

Governance Points

  • Role-specific actions to limit operational mistakes
  • Status-based transitions to enforce process discipline
  • Event logs for each decision and update
