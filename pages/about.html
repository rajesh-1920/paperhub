Product Vision

PaperHub is designed to modernize document-heavy operations for institutions and organizations. The platform focuses on security, transparency, and high operational efficiency across the full document lifecycle.

Business Challenges Solved

  • Missing document visibility in manual operations
  • Version confusion across distributed teams
  • Low accountability in multi-role approval processes
  • Revenue risk from unverified payment triggers

Platform Objectives

  1. Deliver a secure, role-driven document operations platform
  2. Standardize review and approval workflows at scale
  3. Enable payment only after verified pre-approval checkpoints
  4. Maintain complete audit trails for governance and compliance
