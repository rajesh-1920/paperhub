Document Management

Applicants can upload files in supported formats with version-aware storage and metadata tracking.

Approval Pipeline

Each submission follows a defined path from draft to submission, review, pre-approval, payment, and final confirmation.

Role-Based Access Control

The platform enforces access boundaries for admins, officers, and applicants to protect sensitive data and reduce unauthorized actions.

Payment Safety Layer

Payment is only enabled after document pre-approval, helping institutions avoid invalid transactions and reducing financial disputes.

Audit and Reporting

Every major action is timestamped and logged for monitoring, compliance checks, and administrative reporting.
