## Current Theme Specifications

### Color Palette

- **Primary Blue**: `#0284c7` (User-facing, main CTAs)
- **Secondary Cyan**: `#06b6d4` (Hover states, accents)
- **Dark Navy**: `#0f172a` (Text, backgrounds)
- **Light Slate**: `#f8fafc` (Card backgrounds)
- **Slate Gray**: `#64748b` (Muted text)
- **Role Colors**:
  - **Admin**: Teal `#0f766e`
  - **Officer**: Orange `#c2410c`
  - **User**: Blue `#0284c7`

### Design System Elements

1. **Gradients**: All use 135deg direction with rgba transparency
2. **Shadows**: Multi-layer shadows for depth (soft/medium/strong)
3. **Borders**: 1px solid with rgba colors for subtlety
4. **Border Radius**:
   - Buttons/pills: 0.75-0.875rem
   - Cards: 1.25rem
   - Small elements: 0.65rem
5. **Backdrop Filter**: `blur(12px)` for glass-morphism effects

### Typography

- **Font Family**: Inter, Segoe UI, sans-serif
- **Headings**: Sora, Inter (font-weight: 800, letter-spacing: -0.02em)
- **Font Smoothing**: -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased

### Animation & Transitions

- **Smooth Transitions**: `0.25s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)` (standard)
- **Hover Effects**: translateY(-2px to -6px), shadow intensification
- **Pseudo-elements**: ::before for gradients, ::after for decorative lines
